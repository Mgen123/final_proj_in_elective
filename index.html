Mission

The mission of the Otaku University Student Government is to represent the student body, advocate for their needs, and foster a vibrant, inclusive campus culture through leadership, service, and collaboration.

Vision

Our vision is to empower students to become active participants in shaping campus life, promoting transparency, equity, and innovation in all aspects of governance and student engagement.

History

Founded in 2010, the Otaku University Student Government has grown from a small council into a dynamic organization that champions student rights, organizes campus-wide events, and collaborates with faculty to improve academic and social life. Its legacy continues to shape the university's inclusive and creative culture.
